About 2019 International Symposium of Materials for Biomedical Application (2019 ISMBA)

On behalf of the 2019 International Symposium of Materials for Biomedical Application (2019 ISMBA), we would like to cordially invite you to participate in the forthcoming symposium which will be held on December 9-11, 2019, in Chang Gung University, Tao-Yuan, Taiwan, ROC.

The aims of the symposium are to explore the materials technology for biomedical application that includes Nano-Technology; Biotechnology; Supercritical Technology; 3D Printing Technology; Biomedical Surface Engineering; Biomaterials; Nature Polymer; Polymer for Biomedical Application, Metal for Biomedical Application, Ceramic Materials for Biomedical Application, Tissue Engineering; Regenerative Medicine; Nanomedicine; Drug and Gene Delivery; Biosensors and Bioelectronics. We believe that the symposium will give you a good opportunity for fruitful harvest. Moreover, it will be a forum for interdisciplinary discussion between scientists, bioengineers, clinicians, regulatory officials and so on.

We hope that this symposium will be a milestone to the next millennium. Aside from the technical aspects of the conference, we hope you have opportunities to explore the cultural and historical richness of Tao-Yuan. The cultural diversity of Taoyuan County is paralleled by an equal abundance of natural attractions, earning the county the nicknames of the "flower kingdom". The success of the symposium will rely on your contribution and participation. We would like to welcome you all to Taoyuan in December 2019.

Sincerely Yours
