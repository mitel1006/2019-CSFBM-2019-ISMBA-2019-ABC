About 19th Asian BioCeramics Symposium (2019ABC)

ABC Symposium has been held yearly since 2001. The scope of 2019ABC is to gather researchers, clinicians, engineers and students from Asia and all over the world to discuss recent scientific developments and applications of ceramics in medicine. 2019ABC will focus on the latest and exciting innovations in nano-technology, biotechnology, supercritical technology, 3D printing technology, biomedical surface engineering, bioactive glass, bio-inert ceramic materials, biomaterials, tissue engineering, regenerative medicine, nanomedicine, drug and gene delivery, biosensors and bioelectronics. The wide spectrum of topics offers a unique opportunity for scholars across the globe to meet, network, and perceive new scientific innovations. We cordially welcome all the distinguished researchers and delegates to participate in this upcoming symposium, witness invaluable scientific discussions, and contribute to future innovations in the bioceramics fields.
